Precautions to Control Pollution

Avoid Smoking

Smoking contributes to air pollution. Avoid smoking indoors and in areas with poor air circulation.

Reduce Vehicle Emissions

Use public transportation, carpool, or choose eco-friendly vehicles to reduce emissions.

Conserve Energy

Use energy-efficient appliances and turn off lights and electronics when not in use to reduce energy consumption.
